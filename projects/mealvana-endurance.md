Mealvana Endurance provides personalized fueling strategies for runners, cyclists, and triathletes. Built on sports nutrition science and powered by AI optimization.

## Key Features

- **AI-Powered Nutrition Plans** - Personalized fueling strategies powered by advanced AI and linear programming optimization, tailored to your race distance, pace, and preferences.

- **Science-Based Calculations** - Built on ACSM energy expenditure formulas and evidence-based sports nutrition research from leading endurance athlete studies.

- **Personalized Food Preferences** - Tell us what you like, dislike, and what you're willing to try. Your nutrition plan respects your taste preferences and dietary needs.

- **Phase-Specific Guidance** - Separate recommendations for pre-run fueling (1-4 hours before), during-run nutrition, and post-run recovery to optimize performance.

- **Gut Training Support** - Gradually increase carbohydrate intake based on your gut training level from beginner to advanced endurance athlete.

- **Race Day Calendar** - Track your upcoming races and training runs with an integrated calendar that helps you plan your nutrition timeline.

- **Offline-First Design** - All your data stays on your device with full offline functionality. No internet required once you've created your plan.

- **Weather-Aware Hydration** - Sodium and fluid recommendations adjust based on temperature, humidity, and your personal sweat rate.

## Technical Highlights

Built with Flutter for cross-platform performance, Mealvana Endurance uses linear programming algorithms to optimize nutrition plans within your macro targets while respecting your food preferences. The app features local-first data storage for privacy and offline functionality.
