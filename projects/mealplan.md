Mealplan.com is a lifestyle and food-focused website designed to help people with everyday meal preparation. With the tagline "Every tiny thing you need to know to put dinner on the table," the site emphasizes food as a universal connector.

## Content Areas

- **Guides** - Step-by-step cooking guides and meal prep tutorials
- **Resources** - Tools and tips for efficient meal planning
- **Health** - Nutrition information and healthy eating advice
- **Lifestyle** - Food culture and culinary inspiration
- **Reviews** - Product and recipe reviews

## Design Philosophy

The site is built on the belief that "Food is our common ground, a universal experience." We aim to make cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of skill level.

## Technical Implementation

Built with Webflow for rapid development and easy content management. The CMS-powered architecture allows for scalable content creation and SEO optimization.

## Location

Based in Birmingham, Alabama, Mealplan.com serves a growing community of home cooks looking to simplify their dinner routines.
