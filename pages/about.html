About Me

Hi, I'm Lee Martin

I'm a Full-Stack Developer based in Birmingham, Alabama, with a passion for building apps that simplify daily life. With a background from Duke University and Georgia Tech, I bring a strong foundation in computer science and engineering to every project.

My work focuses on creating intuitive, user-friendly applications that solve real problems. From meal planning apps that take the stress out of dinner decisions to community platforms that help people connect, I believe technology should make life easier, not more complicated.

I specialize in mobile development with Flutter and web development with Next.js. I'm also experienced with Webflow for rapid website development and various backend technologies including Firebase and AWS.
